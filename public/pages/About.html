Who We Are

At EduFund, we believe that every student deserves the tools to succeed. We are an online platform that connects students in Egypt and across the Arab world with the educational support they need — whether it's a scholarship, a training course, or essential study materials.

Our Vision

Our vision is simple yet powerful: to build a future where no student is held back by financial limitations. We imagine a world where every learner has equal access to opportunities that allow them to grow, achieve, and thrive in their academic journey.

Our Mission

We are on a mission to break down financial barriers to education by linking students with compassionate donors and sponsors. Through a transparent and user-friendly process, EduFund empowers learners to focus on what truly matters — their education — while we take care of making support reachable and reliable.

Why Choose EduFund?

  • ✔️ A student-first platform focused on real educational needs.
  • ✔️ Simple, fast, and transparent application process.
  • ✔️ Various support types: scholarships, courses, and learning resources.
  • ✔️ Built with trust, transparency, and long-term impact in mind.
